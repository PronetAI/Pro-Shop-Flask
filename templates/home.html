Car Physics Sandbox

This is a sample piece of code that you can download to get an idea of the programs we make here at pronet.

It is a simple car physics sandbox made in pygame. You can control a car using the arrow keys on

your keyboard. There is drift, drag, and even a brake time for the amount of time it takes a car to slow down and come

to a full stop. I hope you enjoy this program and if you have any feedback or suggestions, please email us at pronetaicorp@gmail.com

Make sure to install all of the needed dependencies for this program: os, pygame, pygame.locals, pygame.math and math
